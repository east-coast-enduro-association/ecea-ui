Right on Lewis Rd to Camp! 

This route will be arrowed and please follow  as GPS may lead you on extremely narrow seasonal roads. 

We have a 600 rider limit and are expecting this to sell out. This the first STER’s event that will not require timekeeping; it will be a closed course, start control format Enduro. The HS rider base in this area is massive (as proven at Watkins GNCC) and they will show up for this one.  

This year is our 50th anniversary and the 250th anniversary of our Country the club fully expects this to be the race of the century.  

We changed parking location (2ish miles down the road) to accommodate everyone.  This change gave us the ability to check in and out right from camp for the first 3 sections of the day. 

We have added about 15 miles of brand new trail and oh myyyy is it an absolute blast.  I’ve ridden it all 4 times already with groups of club members and ANEC guys and all of them can’t believe how absolutely awesome these new trails turned out. 

We are upping our game for refreshments at the finish for the bigger turn out 🍻. 

Youth National Harescramble style racing on Saturday!!

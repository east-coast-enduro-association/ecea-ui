A 2-day family fun weekend! September 5th & 6th, 2026. Bring your camping gear, enjoy the campground, and ride the trails. Kids and beginner friendly sections. Hard core enduro for the needy...

There's something for all skill levels here so bring the whole family!

Approx 35 miles of trail. Separate under 12 kids section.
Hare Scramble Loops, Enduro Trail, Hard Enduro transfer sections, Logging Trail/roads...

Food all weekend, Relaxing setting, ride at your pace with friends and family.

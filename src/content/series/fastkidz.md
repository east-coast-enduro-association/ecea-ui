## What is FastKIDZ?

FastKIDZ is ECEA's youth racing program designed specifically for young riders ages 4-15. It provides a safe, structured environment for kids to learn off-road motorcycle racing while having fun and building lifelong skills.

## Program Highlights

- **Age-Appropriate Classes**: Divisions based on age and skill level
- **Shorter Courses**: Scaled-down tracks suitable for younger riders
- **Safety First**: Emphasis on proper technique and safety equipment
- **Family Friendly**: Parents are encouraged to participate and support

## Age Groups

- **Pee Wee**: Ages 4-6
- **Junior**: Ages 7-11
- **Youth**: Ages 12-15

## What You Need

- Youth-sized off-road motorcycle appropriate for rider's age/size
- [AMA membership](https://www.americanmotorcyclist.com/membership/) (youth membership available)
- [ECEA FastKIDZ membership](https://www.moto-tally.com/ECEA/ECEA_PWY/SeriesRegistration.aspx) or day pass (available at registration)
- Full safety gear including:
  - DOT-approved helmet
  - Boots
  - Chest protector
  - Gloves
  - Goggles
  - Knee/elbow guards (recommended)

## Getting Started

FastKIDZ is perfect for young riders who want to try racing. Many of today's top ECEA racers started in the FastKIDZ program. Come out and see what it's all about!

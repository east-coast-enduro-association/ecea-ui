Due to the weather predictions, we are making a few changes to the 2026 ECEA Awards Banquet schedule. Please remember that there are not refunds on the event. If you cannot attend but you have awards, please contact members of your club or other friends to collect your award.

3:30pm 100% Club Awards

4:30pm Arai Helmet Seminar

5:00pm Cocktail Hour

6:00pm Dinner
